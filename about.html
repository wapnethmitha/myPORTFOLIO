About Me

Hello! My name is W. A. P. Nethmitha. I’m currently a second-year undergraduate student pursuing a Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science at the University of Westminster, UK, through IIT Sri Lanka. While I’m dedicated to my studies in the IT field, I have a variety of interests and goals that shape who I am.

My Academic Journey

During my first year at university, I built a strong foundation in several key areas of computer science, including Java programming, HTML, CSS, and basic Python. These courses introduced me to the world of software development, and I’m constantly enhancing my knowledge in these areas. I’ve also gained critical problem-solving skills through various projects and assignments that have shaped my thinking as an aspiring IT professional.

Interests Beyond IT

In addition to my studies, I have a strong passion for aviation. Ever since childhood, I've been captivated by airplanes and the complexities of flight. This fascination drives me to learn more about the aviation industry and its technological advancements, as I believe there's a unique intersection between technology and aviation that can lead to exciting opportunities.

Moreover, I have a keen interest in the broader aspects of business and entrepreneurship, particularly in fields like food and hospitality. The idea of creating a venture that brings people together is intriguing to me, and I look forward to exploring opportunities for my own business in the future.

My Goals and Future Plans

While my current focus is on becoming a professional IT expert, I remain open to various paths in my career. I envision applying my knowledge in technology to solve real-world problems and perhaps integrating those skills into a future business venture. My journey is still unfolding, and I’m eager to see where my passion for IT and aviation takes me.
